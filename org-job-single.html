English Teaching for Primary School Students

PartTime
AfterSchool
Evanston, IL, USA

AfterSchool is a small nonprofit organization serving youth in Evanston and surrounding neighborhoods. We provide free after school programming primarily to foreign youth from low income, first generation immigrant families.

We are seeking tutors for our After School Homework Tutoring Program. Tutors will assist students in grades 9-12 with homework, test preparation, and help students develop good study habits, academic skills and knowledge. Each tutor will work with 5-6 students each shift. We are seeking tutors with strong personal academic records and enthusiasm and passion for teaching!
